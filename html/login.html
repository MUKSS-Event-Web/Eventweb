MUKSS

MUKSS is a non-profit organisation, providing all Korean students in Manchester with various types of welfare benefits. To give full constructive support to students studying abroad in Manchester, MUKSS creates the best environment for students to communicate and expand personal networks through academic and cultural events. Furthermore, MUKSS attempts to play an active role in cultural exchanges among international students, while concentrating on spreading Korean culture.
